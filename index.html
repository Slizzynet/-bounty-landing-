Platform Terms

By registering you agree to participate in competitive gameplay challenges. All missions are community-driven. No refunds once a mission is live. Approved claims receive 80% of the bounty. The platform retains a 20% service fee.

$2 Listing Fee
A non-refundable $2 listing fee is added to every bounty posted. This fee covers platform operations and visibility placement.

2.1 Affiliates of Game Publishers and Platforms

This includes affiliates of companies related to games currently or potentially supported on the platform, including but not limited to: Valorant / League of Legends / Riot Games; CS2 / Valve Corporation; Rivals / NetEase Games; Overwatch / Blizzard Entertainment / Activision Blizzard / Microsoft Gaming.
