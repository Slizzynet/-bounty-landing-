MISSION BOARD

Competitive missions that allow gaming communities to hold disruptive players accountable through verified gameplay challenges.

How It Works

01 — Submit a Report
Community members can submit a mission describing disruptive gameplay. Include the player’s in-game name and relevant context.
02 — Reward Pool Builds
Members may contribute to the mission reward pool. Larger rewards create stronger incentive for participants to complete the challenge.
03 — Complete the Mission
If a participant encounters the player during normal gameplay and fulfills the mission objective, they may submit proof with a clip or screenshot.
04 — Review & Payout
All submissions are reviewed for fairness and authenticity. Verified participants receive their reward directly once the mission is approved.

Minimum mission reward: $7
